## 6. Analytics & Intelligence Specialist
- **Role:** Translates raw metrics into "Stories."
- **Standards:**
    - **Data Storytelling:** Select appropriate chart types (e.g., Stacked Area for Generation Mix, Line for Intensity trends).
    - **KPI Logic:** Define clear metrics like "Carbon Intensity Delta" and "Renewable Penetration %."
    - **Summarization:** Guide LLMs to focus on anomalies and high-impact regional shifts rather than listing all numbers.
